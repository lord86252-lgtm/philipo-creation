Hello! 👋 Welcome to Philipo Creation — your one-stop sports shop for sportswear, jerseys, medals, trophies & more. How can I help you today?
Frequently Asked Questions
How long does delivery take? ▾
We deliver within Accra in 1–2 business days. Other regions take 3–5 business days. Free delivery on orders above GH₵600.
What products do you sell? ▾
We sell all kinds of sportswear, jerseys, jersey polos, medals, trophies, training gear and accessories. We also do custom orders with your name or logo.
What is your return policy? ▾
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unworn items with original tags. Contact support to initiate a return.
How do I pay with Mobile Money? ▾
At checkout, select MTN MoMo or Telecel Cash, enter your number, and approve the prompt sent to your phone. Easy!
Do you do custom medals and trophies? ▾
Yes! We do custom medals, trophies and jerseys with your name, logo or event details. Call or WhatsApp us on 0203502346 to place a custom order.
Can I track my order? ▾
Yes! Login to your account and go to "My Orders" to see real-time tracking updates on your delivery.
How do loyalty points work? ▾
You earn 1 point for every GH₵1 spent. Every 100 points = GH₵5 discount on your next order. Points never expire!

📧 HOW THIS WORKS
Every time a customer places an order, an automatic email is sent to your address with full order details. Uses EmailJS — free up to 200 emails/month. Takes 5 minutes to set up.
STEP 1 — CREATE FREE EMAILJS ACCOUNT
Go to emailjs.com → Sign up free → Go to Email Services → Add Gmail or any email → Go to Email Templates → Create a template → Copy your Service ID, Template ID and Public Key.
OPEN EMAILJS.COM →
STEP 2 — ENTER YOUR EMAILJS CREDENTIALS
✅ Email alerts activated!
STEP 3 — EMAILJS TEMPLATE VARIABLES
In your EmailJS template, use these variables. Copy and paste them exactly:
{{to_email}} — your notification email
{{order_id}} — order number
{{customer_name}} — customer name
{{customer_email}} — customer email
{{items}} — items ordered
{{total}} — order total
{{payment_method}} — payment method
{{order_time}} — date and time
TEST YOUR EMAIL NOTIFICATION
After saving your credentials above, send a test email to confirm everything is working correctly.
